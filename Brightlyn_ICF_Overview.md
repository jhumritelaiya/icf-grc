# Brightlyn Integrated Control Framework (ICF)

## Overview
The **Brightlyn Integrated Control Framework (ICF)** is an enterprise-grade compliance and risk management dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive view of an organization's regulatory compliance and cybersecurity posture. It enables security leaders, compliance officers, and auditors to manage controls, track evidence, monitor policies, and perform risk assessments across various industry-standard frameworks.

## Key Frameworks Supported
- **NIS2** (Network and Information Security Directive)
- **DORA** (Digital Operational Resilience Act)
- **ISO 27001** (Information Security Management)
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **ABRO 2026**
- **ISO 20000-1**
- **PCI DSS**
- **CMMI**

## 14 core Domains
The framework categorizes controls into 14 comprehensive domains:
1. Governance & Enterprise Oversight
2. Risk Management
3. Policy & Document Management
4. Legal & Regulatory Management
5. Third-Party & Supply Chain
6. Cyber Security
7. Cloud & Infrastructure Security
8. Identity & Access Management
9. Data Protection & Privacy
10. Incident & Crisis Management
11. Business Continuity & DR
12. Audit & Assurance
13. Training & Awareness
14. Monitoring & Continuous Improvement

## Key Features
- **Compliance Dashboard:** Real-time visibility into control states (Compliant, Partial Gaps, Critical Gaps) with trend analysis and framework-specific compliance tracking.
- **Control Library:** Centralized repository of controls mapped to regulatory clauses, objectives, implementation steps, and ownership.
- **Policy Management:** Version-controlled policy suite tracking approval status, attestation rates, and review cycles.
- **Evidence Tracking:** Lifecycle management of evidence artifacts (policies, penetration test reports, scan results) supporting regulatory compliance.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Granular access management based on roles including Super Admin, Compliance Officer, Security Manager, Internal Auditor, and Executive Viewer.
- **Audit Workflow:** Detailed audit logging and formal status change workflows with required justifications for compliance exceptions or lifecycle state transitions.

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend Framework:** React (Vite)
- **Styling & UI:** Custom CSS-in-JS design tokens, Lucide React (Icons)
- **Data Visualization:** Recharts (Line Charts, Radar Charts, Pie Charts, Area Charts)
